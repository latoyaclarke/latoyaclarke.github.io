Latoya Clarke

Latoya Clarke is an experienced web manager in Brooklyn, NY. She’s helped many organizations transform their digital presence with meaningful content and optimal UX design.

Portfolio

Over the last decade, Latoya has worked for nonprofit and educational organizations to ensure their web presence and content effectively get their message out. She’s been on the front lines of email marketing, frontend development and, more recently, positioned in internal business communications.

Website Design

Butterflies USA

Butterflies USA is a state-side charity that supports street and working children’s rights in India. After their sister organization, Butterflies NGO, underwent a website refresh, they rebranded their digital presence. This site is built in WordPress with ACF extensions; it is an independent template coded from scratch.

National Center for Security & Preparedness

After fortunate circumstances, the National Center for Security & Preparedness had the opportunity to develop a digital brand separate from its University partner. As a result, site content was migrated from the defunct RedDot CMS to a dynamic PHP framework. New branding included logos, banners, and layouts for print and digital content.

Digits Therapy

Cuteness loading! Digits Therapy is an occupational therapy practice that empowers the kid in us all. This static one-page site is built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Content organized in WCAG-compliant sections.
